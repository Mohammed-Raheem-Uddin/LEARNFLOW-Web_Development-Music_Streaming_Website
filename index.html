about singer

Atif Aslam

Atif Aslam (born 12 March 1983) is a Pakistani singer, songwriter and actor. He has recorded many songs in both Pakistan and India, and is known for his vocal belting technique. Born in Wazirabad, his musical career began in 2003 as part of the band Jal.
